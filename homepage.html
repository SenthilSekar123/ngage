the world's best hospitality summit

The Hotelier Summit is a global hospitality summit that brings together industry leaders and veterans. With over 5000 people attending this event, every year, in their region - we’ve seen projects move from pen and paper to conceptualization.

With 19 shows conducted successfully, we’ve seen great benefits when stakeholders meet in person. The stakeholders - decision-makers, specifiers and influencers - come to Hotelier Summit to talk business.
